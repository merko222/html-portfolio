The Best Anime According To Meher

My top three anime of all time

Yona Of The Dawn

This is one of the best anime I've watched. The plot, charcters, design, all of it is amazing. It would be my comfort anime if I didn't need comforting after watching it

Fullmetal Alchemist Brotherhood

A very good vs evil kinda thing goes on here, except we don't know the evil here. All in all, the pacing and plot is pretty well thought out, so its a must recommend.

Yuri ON Ice!!!

Now THIS is a comfort anime. The main character finds a way out of his problems and finds family and love on the way. The only villain here is MAPPA for cancelling Ice Adolesence.
